Chief Mouser Infographic

In the United Kingdom, the Prime Minister's office at 10 Downing St. employs cats to control the building's rodent problem. As a cat-lover and owner, I found the concept of the animals holding office in the government to be fascinating and amusing, and decided to research the subject and put my findings into an infographic.

Medium: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop.
