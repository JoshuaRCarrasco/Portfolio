Merch Designs

One of my favorite things to do in Adobe Illustrator is take a figure from pop culture- a character from a movie or video game, an actor I like, etc- and make a vector illustration of them. I like to challenge myself to represent the figure as simply as I can, mostly using simple shapes and flat colors. One reason for this challenge is that I believe the simple, clean style makes these designs ideal for various merchandise such as t-shirts and phone cases.

Medium: Adobe Illustrator.
