Gig Flyer For Boston Bands

When I became a designer, my background as a musician is one of the first things that informed my work. Album covers, concert posters, and even the outfits of rock stars like David Bowie and Prince are rich areas of inspiration. So naturally one of the first things I wanted to design was a flyer for a local show. I chose the concept of a cartoonish alien invasion to fit with the style of the bands, which is simple, quirky, and fun, and the buildings are based on a street from the town the venue is in.

Medium: Adobe Illustrator.
