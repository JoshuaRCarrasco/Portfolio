Whiskey Infographic

Whiskey is a spirit deeply rooted in tradition, with several different kinds each having their own rules and even laws governing how they're made. Not knowing much about the different kinds, I decided to do some research and then put my findings into this infographic. I chose mostly brown colors to represent not only the color of whiskey but also the barrels used to age it and the woody, earthy flavor it can often have.

Medium: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop.
