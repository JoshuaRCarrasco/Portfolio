Infographic Designs

Infographics are a great way to share knowledge with people in a way that's engaging and enjoyable. When designing these infographics, I wanted to explore subjects I liked but knew little about and use my own designing style to show my findings. With the Whiskey infographic I chose a brown color scheme to represent not only the color of whiskey but also the woody, earthy flavor it can often have. With the Chief Mouser infographic, I based it on the colors of the Union Jack, the UK's national flag.

Medium: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop.
