Merch Designs

As a craft beer enthusiast, I thought designing my own beer can labels would be a great way to get some experience with packaging design. My goal was to create eye-catching looks that would stand out on a shelf, because while the flavor of beer is the most important part, the label is often the first impression!

Medium: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop.
